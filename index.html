About Me

I am Priyanka Majethiya, a PhD student in Mathematical Sciences at SVNIT, Surat, working under the guidance of Dr. Shivam Bajpeyi. My research focuses on approximation theory, neural network operators, and the sampling and reconstruction of signals in different function spaces.
