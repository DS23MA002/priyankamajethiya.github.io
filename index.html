About Me

I am Priyanka Majethiya, a PhD student in the Department of Mathematics at SVNIT, working under the guidance of Dr. Shivam Bajpeyi. My research focuses on the approximation properties of neural network operators, as well as the sampling and reconstruction of signals in various function spaces.
